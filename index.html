Introduction

Our project explores the relationship between seasonal changes and the popularity of various music genres on Spotify. Music consumption is deeply personal and culturally significant and is often influenced by factors like mood, weather, and social activities, all of which are tied to seasonal shifts. By understanding how different genres perform throughout the year, we can gain insights into consumer behavior, allowing for tailored marketing strategies, personalized playlists, and deeper engagement with users. This topic is not only relevant to the music industry but also holds value in fields such as data science because once these patterns are observed, larger societal trends can be revealed. By using data visualization, we aim to identify genre-specific fluctuations over time. This analysis will provide valuable insights into how seasonal shifts influence digital consumption habits. These insights will not only be helpful to optimize content delivery but also to improve personalized recommendations and enhance the user experience.
