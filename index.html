How Seasons and Weather Influence Spotify Users' Listening Habits

Data Visualizations Summary

Introduction

Our project explores the relationship between seasonal changes and the popularity of various music genres on Spotify. Music consumption is deeply personal and culturally significant, influenced by factors like mood, weather, and social activities tied to seasonal shifts. By understanding how genres perform throughout the year, we aim to uncover consumer behavior insights that could lead to tailored marketing strategies, personalized playlists, and deeper user engagement.
